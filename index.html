Digitalizing Akira Minagawa

During the secound week in RCA I found the book Mina pherhonen 3 print in a old artbook store. Akira Minagawa is famous for his insistence of using hand-drawing process for his textile design. I created algorithm to try to recreate the textile texture of his design from purely 0s and 1s, questioning the possibility of digital media and ask the following questions: Can we recreate everything digitally? If so, does it also means that the “real” world we are in can also be another kind of “digital”?
